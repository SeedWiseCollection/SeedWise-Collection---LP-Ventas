Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

SeedWise ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding that data. This policy applies to all visitors and customers of seedwisecollection.com (the "Site").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Purchase Information: When you buy a Product, our payment processor (Gumroad) collects your name, email address, billing address, and payment details. We receive your name and email address to fulfill your order and provide customer support. We do not store credit card numbers or full payment details.
  • Email Subscription: If you subscribe to our email list through our lead magnet form (powered by MailerLite), we collect your email address and, optionally, your first name. This data is stored by MailerLite on our behalf.
  • Customer Support: If you contact us at hello@seedwisecollection.com, we retain the content of your message and your email address to respond to your inquiry and improve our support.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors interact with our Site. This may include aggregated, anonymized data such as pages visited, time on site, referral source, and device type. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
  • Cookies: Our Site uses a minimal number of cookies as described in our Cookies Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Deliver purchased Products to you via email or download link through Gumroad.
  • Send you the lead magnet guide you requested ("The 4 Ways to Raise Financially Confident Kids") and subsequent welcome emails.
  • Process refund requests under our 30-day "Plant It or Return It" guarantee.
  • Respond to your questions, feedback, or support requests.
  • Improve our Site, Products, and customer experience based on aggregated usage patterns.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (for EEA/UK Visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal basis for collecting and using your personal information includes: performance of a contract (to deliver Products you purchased), legitimate interests (to improve our Site and communicate with you), consent (for email marketing, which you can withdraw at any time), and compliance with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with data brokers or advertising networks. We may share data only with:

  • Gumroad, Inc.: Our payment processor, which handles transactions and delivers digital Products under their own Privacy Policy.
  • MailerLite: Our email marketing platform, which stores subscriber data (email address, name) under their own Privacy Policy. MailerLite processes data in accordance with GDPR.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, regulation, subpoena, or legal process.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Specifically:

  • Purchase records: Retained as long as needed for accounting, tax, and legal compliance (typically 7 years for financial records in the US).
  • Email subscriber data: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Support correspondence: Retained for up to 2 years after your last interaction, then deleted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@seedwisecollection.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Products are designed for parents and guardians to use with their children. Our Site and purchase process are intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all Site traffic, secure third-party processors (Gumroad, MailerLite) with their own security certifications, and limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. Our service providers (Gumroad, MailerLite) may process data in the US or EU. Where applicable, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

10. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at hello@seedwisecollection.com.

11. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Material changes will be communicated via email to existing subscribers.
