This Cookies Policy explains what cookies and similar tracking technologies are, how SeedWise ("we," "us," "our") uses them on seedwisecollection.com (the "Site"), and what choices you have regarding their use.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, understand how you interact with pages, and enable certain features. Cookies are widely used across the internet and are an essential part of how modern websites function.

Cookies can be "session" cookies (which are deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

3. Cookies We Do NOT Use

SeedWise is committed to a clean, privacy-respecting experience. We do not use:

  • Social media tracking pixels (Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, etc.).
  • Cross-site tracking cookies.
  • Cookies that sell or share your data with data brokers or ad networks.
  • Fingerprinting or device-identification technologies.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only first-party cookies, or alert you when a cookie is being set. Please note that if you disable essential cookies, some features of our Site (such as the checkout process) may not function properly.

To manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

5. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Our Site respects Do Not Track signals sent by your browser. When a DNT signal is detected, we disable any non-essential tracking cookies.
